Dichang
Zhang

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Dimitris Samaras. I also collaborate with Prof. Simon Birrer in the Department of Physics and Astronomy. I have also been a visiting doctoral student at EPFL with Prof. Pascal Fua. Before that, I completed my B.S. in Data Science and Honors Computer Science at the University of Michigan, where I worked with Prof. David Fouhey. I am currently a research intern at Futurewei Technologies.

Research interests: computer vision, machine learning, and astronomy image analysis.
